Frequently Ask Questions about natural disasters
Q: How many natural disasters were there in 2023?

In 2023, the U.S. experienced 89 severe natural disasters with 28 natural disasters costing over $1 billion.

Q: What's the number one natural disaster?

In 1931, as many as 3.7 million people were killed in the Central China floods when the Yangtze River overflowed.

Q: What percentage of natural disasters are earthquakes?

Earthquakes happen every day across the world and since most do not meet the criteria of a natural disaster (economic loss of 50 million U.S. dollars; insured loss of 25 million U.S. dollars; ten fatalities; 50 injured; or 2,000 homes or structures damaged), earthquakes make up only a small percentage of natural disasters.

Q: What state has had the most disasters?

From 1980 to 2023, Texas has experienced the most natural disasters, according to Climate.gov.
